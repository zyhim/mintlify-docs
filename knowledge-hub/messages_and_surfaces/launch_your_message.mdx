Launching a message is the culmination of your messaging strategy. It involves finalizing your content, confirming your targeting, and initiating delivery to your selected audience.

## Launch Checklist

- Content review: Ensure all message components are complete and error-free
- Audience confirmation: Verify that your targeting is correctly set up
- Scheduling verification: Confirm delivery times and frequency settings
- Testing: Send test messages to ensure proper rendering across devices
- Analytics setup: Make sure tracking is in place to measure results

With aampe's platform, launching messages is streamlined and intelligent. The system continuously learns from each message's performance to improve future campaigns, making your messaging strategy more effective over time.
