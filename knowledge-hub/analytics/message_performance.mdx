The Live Messages View helps you track the performance of all your messages in real-time, letting you easily compare key metrics like CTR, conversion events, and engagement success.

- Identify which messages are driving the most revenue, clicks, and engagement.
- Monitor Freshness scores to avoid user fatigue and keep content dynamic.
